Density Field Dynamics — Historical Lineage & Refractive Density Index

Historical ancestry of refractive, density-dependent gravity and optics: from early gradient-medium intuition (Kepler, Descartes), through Fermat’s least time and Einstein’s 1911–1912 variable-c program, to a modern scalar-field proposal: Density Field Dynamics (DFD). The figures below present both a chronological timeline and a heuristic ranking by conceptual completeness.

DFD (Alcock, 2024–): a single scalar field ψ on flat ℝ³ sets the optical index n = eψ and the test-mass acceleration a = (c²/2)∇ψ, reproducing GR’s classic optical and 1PN tests and motivating sector-resolved LPI experiments as decisive laboratory discriminators.
