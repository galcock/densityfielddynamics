DFD — Historical Lineage & Refractive Density Index

A compact ancestry of refractive, density-dependent gravity/optics — from early gradient-medium intuition (Kepler, Descartes), through Fermat’s least time and Einstein’s 1911–12 variable-c, to a modern, falsifiable field proposal: Density Field Dynamics (DFD). Two views below: a chronological timeline and a heuristic ranking by conceptual completeness.

DFD (Alcock, 2024–): one scalar ψ on flat ℝ³ sets the optical index n = eψ and matter acceleration a = (c²/2)∇ψ, recovering GR’s classic optical/1PN tests and motivating decisive sector-resolved LPI experiments.
