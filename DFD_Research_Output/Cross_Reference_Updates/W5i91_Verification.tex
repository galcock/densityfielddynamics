\item \textbf{Swampland}: New paragraph in Section V discusses DFD's relationship to the swampland conjectures. DFD satisfies the distance conjecture (the density field has a natural metric on moduli space) and the de Sitter conjecture is automatically satisfied because DFD's dark energy is not a cosmological constant.
