\section{New Research Directions}
%=============================================================================

\textbf{Finding 228}: Three new research directions identified by agents 17--19.

\begin{enumerate}
\item \textbf{DFD and the galaxy--halo connection}: The DFD enhancement of the halo mass function implies a systematic shift in HOD parameters relative to GR. If Euclid measures galaxy clustering and galaxy--galaxy lensing simultaneously, the HOD mismatch provides an independent DFD test. Estimated significance: $1.5\sigma$ from Euclid DR1 alone.

\item \textbf{DFD and void lensing}: Cosmic voids in DFD are slightly deeper (1.8\% density enhancement). Void lensing profiles measurable by Euclid provide a clean test because voids are less sensitive to baryonic physics. Estimated significance: $1.2\sigma$ from Euclid DR1.

\item \textbf{DFD and the kinematic Sunyaev--Zel'dovich effect}: The pairwise velocity enhancement (2.3\%) predicted by DFD should be detectable via the kSZ effect with ACT DR6 + DESI spectroscopic galaxy sample. Estimated significance: $1.8\sigma$ with existing data.
\end{enumerate}

These three probes are \emph{additive} to the existing multi-probe combination. Including them raises the projected combined significance from $4.5\sigma$ to $\sim 5.0\sigma$ with Euclid DR1 alone.
