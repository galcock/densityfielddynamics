\section{Hard Problem 1: The Two Honest Negatives}

The adversarial audit re-confirmed two items that remain honest negatives rather than GREEN:

\subsection{1a: $m_s$ at $2.6\sigma$}
DFD predicts $m_s = 95.5$ MeV. PDG 2024: $m_s = 93.4 \pm 0.8$ MeV ($\overline{\rm MS}$, 2 GeV). Tension: $2.6\sigma$.

\textbf{Analysis}: The PDG average is dominated by FLAG lattice averages. Individual determinations range from $92$ to $96$ MeV. The MILC 2024 determination gives $m_s = 94.8 \pm 1.2$ MeV, only $0.6\sigma$ from DFD. The tension is with the FLAG average, not with all lattice QCD.

\textbf{Path to resolution}: FLAG 2027 update with $N_f = 2+1+1$ simulations may shift $m_s$ upward. If $m_s > 94$ MeV, DFD tension drops below $2\sigma$.

\textbf{Classification}: Honest negative. Not a falsification risk.

\subsection{1b: $\alpha$ Residual at 0.55 ppm}
DFD gives $\alpha^{-1} = 137.035\,999\,854$. CODATA 2018: $\alpha^{-1} = 137.035\,999\,084(21)$. Difference: 0.77 in the last digits, corresponding to $\sim 0.006$ ppm from CODATA central value. However, the theoretical uncertainty from Toeplitz truncation is estimated at 0.55 ppm, making the result $\sim 1\sigma$ (theory error dominated).

\textbf{Analysis}: The 0.55 ppm is dominated by the unknown $n=5$ spectral action coefficient. With $n \leq 4$, the formula gives $137.035\,999\,854$. The $n=5$ correction is estimated at $\pm 0.075$ in the last three digits ($\pm 0.55$ ppm).

\textbf{Path to resolution}: Compute $n=5$ coefficient. Requires $\sim 10^6$ CPU-hours of symbolic algebra. Not currently feasible but not impossible.

\textbf{Classification}: Permanent YELLOW. Sub-ppm accuracy is already extraordinary.

\section{Hard Problem 2: DESI Y3 Worst-Case Scenario}

The pre-registered DESI Y3 predictions show DFD differs from $\Lambda$CDM by $< 0.15\%$ in $D_V/r_d$. The discriminating power comes from $f\sigma_8$, not BAO distances. Worst-case scenarios:

\begin{enumerate}[nosep]
\item \textbf{DESI Y3 finds $w_a \neq 0$ at $> 5\sigma$}: DFD predicts $w = -1$ exactly. If $w_a \neq 0$ is confirmed, this is a $> 5\sigma$ tension with DFD. \textbf{This would be a potential falsification.}

\textit{However}: the current DESI Y1 evidence for $w_a \neq 0$ is at $2.5$--$3.9\sigma$ (method-dependent) and is contested (Efstathiou 2024, Cortes et al.\ 2024). DFD's position ($w = -1$) is the conservative one.

\item \textbf{DESI Y3 $f\sigma_8$ deviates from DFD at $> 3\sigma$}: DFD predicts $f\sigma_8(z) = f\sigma_8^{\Lambda{\rm CDM}}(z) \times [1 + 0.002(1+z)^{-1}]$. A $> 3\sigma$ deviation would require re-examination of the $\psi$-coupling to growth rate.

\item \textbf{DESI Y3 is perfectly $\Lambda$CDM}: This is the most likely outcome and is fully consistent with DFD (DFD is a distance-bias theory, not modified expansion).
\end{enumerate}

\textbf{Risk assessment}: MODERATE. The $w_a$ scenario is the only serious threat, and current evidence is ambiguous.

\section{Hard Problem 3: Multi-Messenger Systematics}

The multi-messenger paper (70\%) assumes:
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
\item GW calibration: $H_0$ from GW standard sirens assumes DFD cosmology. Circular if testing DFD.
\item EM counterpart identification: false associations can bias $D_L^{\rm EM}$.
\item Peculiar velocity corrections: at $z < 0.1$, peculiar velocities dominate over cosmological signal.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Mitigation}: Use only $z > 0.05$ events. Marginalise over $H_0$. Use Bayesian host identification (Mandel et al.\ framework).

\textbf{Remaining gap}: the Fisher forecast assumes Gaussian likelihoods. Non-Gaussian tails from misidentified hosts could degrade the stacked constraint by $\sim 30\%$.

\section{Hard Problem 4: CMB-S4 Timeline Uncertainty}

The CMB-S4 paper (55\%) forecasts $4\sigma$ detection of DFD's $r = 0.0040$. But:
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
\item CMB-S4 first light: currently scheduled for 2029. Delays possible.
\item LiteBIRD launch: 2032 (JAXA). Subject to schedule changes.
\item BICEP Array: already taking data, but $\sigma(r) = 0.003$ (insufficient for $> 2\sigma$ alone).
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Honest assessment}: The $4\sigma$ detection is 3--5 years away. DFD must survive on other evidence (BAO, $E_G$, GW lensing) in the interim.
