\subsubsection{Test 1: The $\kalpha = \alpha^2/(2\pi)$ Relationship}

\textbf{Observable:} Compare a clock to a non-electromagnetic standard
(e.g., gravitational acceleration measurement or atom interferometry).

\begin{equation}
