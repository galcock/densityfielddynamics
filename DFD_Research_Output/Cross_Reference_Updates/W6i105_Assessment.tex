\section{Three Honest Negatives}

\begin{enumerate}
\item \textbf{Born rule derivation is incomplete} (F425).
  The sufficient condition (Proposition 5.1.1 of i103) was identified but
  the core connection between $\rho_i$ and $|c_i|^2$ was not established.
  This is an unsolved problem in quantum foundations generally, not a
  DFD-specific failure. Status: logged, ongoing.

\item \textbf{CKM $\bar\rho = 0.502$ vs.\ PDG $0.159$} (F427).
  A $3\times$ discrepancy in the unitarity-triangle apex. The other
  Wolfenstein parameters agree well, and the Jarlskog invariant matches
  exactly, which constrains the form of any correction. Possible explanations:
  (a)~higher-order overlap corrections, (b)~revised localization geometry,
  (c)~a genuine structural limitation. This is the most concerning tension
  in the A+ Sprint.

\item \textbf{PMNS $\theta_{23}$ shows 10\% discrepancy} (F429).
  DFD predicts near-maximal mixing while data suggests the upper octant.
  The experimental situation is unsettled (DUNE will resolve the octant).
  Not yet classified as a tension --- awaiting data.
\end{enumerate}

\section{Previously Known Honest Negatives (Unchanged)}

\item Bullet Cluster 2--3$\times$ lensing deficit.
\item No peer-reviewed publication.
\item No independent experimental test.

\end{enumerate}
