\item \textbf{CMB $R_{31} = 0.430 \pm 0.003$: MATCHES OBSERVATION (Agent 5, Grade B+)}\\
The refined transfer function calculation including scale-dependent DFD enhancement ($\varepsilon_\psi(k_1) = 0.031$, $\varepsilon_\psi(k_3) = 0.0025$) plus the baryon-loading correction ($+0.003$) gives $R_{31}^{\mathrm{DFD}} = 0.430 \pm 0.003$, matching Planck's $0.430 \pm 0.010$ at $< 0.1\sigma$. This is a major improvement from i52's $0.427$--$0.429$ and the earlier pessimistic $0.41$. Tentative scorecard upgrade: CMB $R_{31}$ from \YELLOW\ to \GREEN.

\textit{Impact}: HIGHEST. Narrows the last major CMB gap. Moves scorecard to 62/4/4. SymBoltz.jl needed for final confirmation.

\item \textbf{$V_{\mathrm{int}} = 4\pi^4$ Chain CLEAN AND UNIQUE (Agent 3, Grade A)}\\
The Dirac quantisation result from i52 propagates through the entire $\alpha$ derivation with zero hidden dependencies. $V_{\mathrm{int}}$ enters only in $K_{\mathrm{geom}}$; $\Tr(Y^2) = 10$ is independent of metric normalisation; the spectral truncation factor uses the same Dirac-quantised metric. Crucially, $V_{\mathrm{int}} = \pi^4$ gives $\alpha^{-1} \approx 34.3$ (wrong) and $V_{\mathrm{int}} = 2\pi^4$ gives $68.5$ (wrong). Only $4\pi^4$ works. This is not circular --- the value comes from topology, not from matching $\alpha$.

\textit{Impact}: HIGH. Eliminates the last ``is this fine-tuned?'' question about the $\alpha$ derivation.

\item \textbf{$\alpha$ Residual: Honest Framing Established (Agent 1/8, Grade A/B)}\\
The $+5.6$ ppb ($+37\sigma$) residual is framed as a missing $a_6$ Seeley--DeWitt sub-leading correction of order $\mathcal{O}(k_{\max}^{-3})$. Agent~8 shows the gauge-sector $a_6$ coefficient with suppression factor $\sim 10^{-3}$ gives $\delta\alpha^{-1} \sim 10^{-7}$, consistent with the residual. The recommended v3.3 statement: ``zero-free-parameter result accurate to 5.6 parts per billion, with the residual attributed to a sub-leading spectral correction.''

\textit{Impact}: HIGH. Provides the definitive framing for the $\alpha$ claim. Neither overclaims nor undersells.

\item \textbf{Scale-Dependent $S_8$ Prediction (Agent 13, Grade A)}\\
DFD predicts a crossover in $S_8(R)$ at $R_\times \approx 8\,h^{-1}$ Mpc, where the transition from Newtonian to MOND regimes projects onto the matter power spectrum. The February 2026 KiDS-Legacy review (arXiv:2602.12238) reports tentative evidence for scale-dependent $S_8$, qualitatively consistent with this prediction. Testable with Euclid first cosmology (October 2026).

\textit{Impact}: HIGH. A unique, quantitative, falsifiable DFD prediction testable within 7 months.

\item \textbf{Sidereal Clock Modulation: Most Testable Prediction (Agent 13, Grade A)}\\
DFD predicts a sidereal variation in optical clock frequency ratios at the $\sim 10^{-16}$ level for clock pairs with large DFD discriminant (e.g., Sr vs Al$^+$). Current clock precision is $\sim 10^{-18}$, placing the predicted signal $\sim 100\times$ above the noise floor. Existing data may already constrain this signal.

\textit{Impact}: HIGH. Immediately testable with existing technology. If the signal is absent in current data, DFD is constrained.

\item \textbf{$c_\tau = \pi/\sqrt{3}$ Partially Derived (Agents 2/6, Grade B)}\\
The monopole harmonic overlap integral on $\CP^2 \times S^3$ gives $c_\tau \approx \pi/\sqrt{5} = 1.405$, not $\pi/\sqrt{3} = 1.814$. The remaining factor $\sqrt{5/3} = 1.291$ is unaccounted for --- likely from the Dirac propagator correction or the $KO$-dimension 6 real structure. The $A_5$ attribution remains \textbf{definitively falsified} (transcendental vs algebraic). The coefficient is geometric, not group-theoretic.

\textit{Impact}: MEDIUM-HIGH. Progress on a key open problem, but incomplete. For v3.3: state the geometric motivation honestly.

\item \textbf{Convention Sensitivity in $V_{\mathrm{int}}$ Derivation (Agent 15, Grade A)}\\
The error hunt found that the Chern--Weil normalisation convention determines whether $V_{\mathrm{int}} = \pi^4$ or $4\pi^4$. Under the algebraic geometry convention ($c_1 = [\omega/(2\pi)]$), the rescaling to $\int_{\CP^1}\omega = 2\pi$ gives Vol$(\CP^2) = 2\pi^2$ and $V_{\mathrm{int}} = 4\pi^4$ (correct). Under the differential geometry convention, no rescaling is needed but Vol$(\CP^2) = \pi^2/2$ (gives wrong $\alpha$). The spectral action uses the algebraic geometry convention.

\textit{Impact}: MEDIUM. The result is correct but v3.3 must include a ``Convention'' box to prevent future confusion.

\item \textbf{Born Rule Downgraded to Consistency Argument (Agent 4, Grade B)}\\
The DFD Born rule argument is a decoherence mechanism via the constraint field, not a derivation from non-quantum axioms. The key gap: Browder--Minty guarantees \emph{uniqueness} of $\psi_i$ for each $\rho_i$, but not \emph{orthogonality} of $\psi_i$ and $\psi_j$. Orthogonality is effectively achieved for macroscopic branches (exponential overlap suppression). Honest v3.3 statement: ``consistency argument,'' not ``derivation.''

\textit{Impact}: MEDIUM. Corrects an overclaim. Does not affect the physics but improves intellectual honesty.

\item \textbf{$c_b = 4/3$ Confirmed Phenomenological (Agent 7, Grade C)}\\
Neither higher-loop perturbative QCD ($\sim 10\%$ at all orders), instantons ($\sim 10^{-13}$), confinement effects ($\sim 7\%$), nor colour-magnetic interactions can produce $c_b = 4/3$ from an $\mathcal{O}(1)$ starting point. Maximum plausible QCD correction: $\sim 15\%$, far short of $33\%$. The coincidence $c_b = C_F = 4/3$ remains unexplained. The ``colour-vertex saturation'' mechanism is not supported by computation.

\textit{Impact}: MEDIUM. Confirms i52 negative result with exhaustive analysis. For v3.3: remove the QCD-on-$S^3$ claim.

\item \textbf{Euclid October 2026: DFD Action Plan (Agent 17, Grade A)}\\
Euclid first cosmology (weak lensing $S_8$, galaxy clustering BAO, galaxy--galaxy lensing) is 7 months away. DFD must: (1) compute DFD halo model $P(k)$ by July; (2) compute $\sigma_8(R)$ curve by August; (3) compute lensing--dynamics ratio by August; (4) write pre-registration paper by September. The halo model approach does NOT require Phase 0A.

\textit{Impact}: STRATEGIC. Failure to publish predictions before Euclid data wastes DFD's best opportunity.

\end{enumerate}
