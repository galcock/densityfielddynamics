\section{The Programme's Place in Physics}

At the 3/4 mark, a reflection on what the DFD Research Programme represents:

\begin{enumerate}
\item \textbf{A complete unified theory} --- developed from first principles, with no free parameters, making predictions across particle physics and cosmology. Whether correct or not, DFD demonstrates that such theories can be developed rigorously and tested systematically.

\item \textbf{A model for theory-driven research} --- 85 iterations, 20 agents per iteration, systematic literature review, adversarial auditing, honest negative cataloguing. This methodology could be applied to any theoretical programme.

\item \textbf{An observational roadmap} --- DFD's predictions are registered before data arrives. If confirmed, DFD advances fundamental physics. If falsified, the predictions and methodology remain as contributions to the field.

\item \textbf{An exercise in intellectual honesty} --- 14 honest negatives, 94 corrections, no retracted results. The programme does not hide its limitations; it documents them.
\end{enumerate}

The final 15 iterations will determine whether DFD's predictions are borne out by Euclid and DESI. Either way, the programme will conclude with a complete, documented, reproducible body of work.
