Cross-check against the BAHAMAS and FLAMINGO simulation suites (GR-based baryonic simulations) confirms that the baryonic feedback amplitude is consistent with state-of-the-art predictions. The DFD-specific baryonic shift is a second-order effect: the primary DFD signal ($\sim 1.5\%$) is modified by $\sim 0.1$--$0.4\%$ from baryonic physics.
