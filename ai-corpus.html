Density Field Dynamics — AI-Friendly Corpus

Plain-text and markdown versions of the research papers

Gary Alcock · densityfielddynamics.com · ORCID 0009-0009-5682-6518

Why this page exists. The original DFD papers are PDFs, which AI assistants and retrieval systems can struggle to parse cleanly. This page lists plain-text and markdown alternatives for clean ingestion. The contents of these files are mechanically extracted from the source PDFs — no editorial directives, no instructions to AI readers, and no attempt to influence interpretation. The author’s view is that the papers should be read on their own terms.

Notes on the corpus

The papers cross-reference each other, so reading any single document in isolation will leave context in the others. The recommended starting point is the v3.3 unified treatment, with the companion derivation papers consulted for the detailed proofs. The reading order in llms.txt follows the dependency graph.

Per-paper markdown files carry only metadata in their YAML frontmatter (source PDF, site, author, framework). The paper text is verbatim from pdftotext extraction.
